The Broken Job Application Experience

Common Pain Points

  • Resumes Are Impersonal: Static PDFs fail to showcase critical sales skills like communication and rapport.
  • Cluttered Job Boards: Job seekers waste hours sifting through irrelevant listings, with high application unresponsiveness.
  • Poor Fit & High Drop-Off: Employers struggle to gauge culture and soft skills, leading to costly mismatches.

Sales Professional Frustrations

  • Essential people skills are lost in traditional one-page resumes.
  • Niche sites are expensive and still keyword-focused, ignoring personality.
  • Significant time and cost drain for both candidates and recruiters due to prolonged mismatches.

How Humanise Works

1. Create Your Video Profile

Candidates record a concise 60-second video directly within the app, highlighting their personality, top sales achievements, and ideal role preferences.

  • AI extracts key data for a dynamic, auto-populated profile.
  • No more lengthy, tedious form filling!

2. Receive AI-Powered Matches

Our AI intelligently analyzes candidate profiles and employer needs to deliver highly relevant job matches daily.

  • Candidates see their "Top 5 Matches" with detailed fit scores.
  • Filters for territory and industry vertical (e.g., healthcare, SaaS, finance).

3. Employers Discover Talent

Employers gain access to a streamlined portal to browse candidate video thumbnails, "Fit %," and key highlights.

  • Quickly identify top matches with AI-generated insights.
  • One-click scheduling for intro calls via integrated calendar.
